OUR ROBOTS

World-leading Level 4 autonomy robots

Starship robots are 99% autonomous. They learn with every journey – and they’ve safely completed millions more deliveries than any competitor.

Our robots deliver hot food, groceries and industrial supplies, using a combination of radars, cameras, sensors and machine learning to identify objects and navigate the world around them.
